Community

Chick-Fil-A Greeley as well as every other CFA are heavily involved in the communties that they are in. Every location is independently own and operated, so really the operators can choose which local orginzations they would like to donate to. CFA Greeley also does free meals for Veteran's day for veterans!

Chick-Fil-A as a franchise also donates food to the Salvation Army almost weekley and every location is encouraged to do so as well.
